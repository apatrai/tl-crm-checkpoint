03 · WHY WE WIN

Distribution and friction, not features

The audience is already ours

Thousands of active workspaces with no CRM connected see it in-app. No cold launch — that's the difference from MessageHub.

The risky half already exists

WhatsApp connect, session sync and the billing rail are reused from the platform (the seam 10+ integrations were built on). Net-new: the CRM app itself — sized honestly after the Calypso prototype.

Monthly, self-serve

Kommo — the market leader — only sells 6–24-month upfront contracts. We sell $19/month, cancel anytime, no sales call.

05 · THE BET

Small bet, mechanical gate

$19/mo
per workspace, 3 seats, +$5/seat — a starting anchor, validated by three price tests in the pilot. Free for paying TimelinesAI customers — retention first.
S → M
small prototype on Calypso (our rapid-build platform), then a medium production build — sizing re-checked after the prototype. No new brand, no paid acquisition.
2 of 3
new WhatsApp-activated workspaces have no CRM — the majority of inflow becomes sellable-to, and the leakiest cohort gets a reason to stay. That's the prize.

Scale if (90 days)

  • ≥100 activated workspaces
  • retention lift in the activated no-CRM cohort vs matched control
  • ≥20 paying / ≥$500 MRR on the charged cohort (trialing + churned — a secondary signal; the paying base is free by design)

Park if

  • <30 activated workspaces, or
  • <8 paying with a flat trajectory
  • in-between → one 45-day extension with a named fix, then it's mechanical
