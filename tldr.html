03 · WHY WE WIN

Distribution and friction, not features

The audience is already ours

Thousands of active workspaces with no CRM connected see it in-app. No cold launch — that's the difference from MessageHub.

The platform already exists

Same SDK as our CRM integrations — 10+ shipped in the past month. WhatsApp connect, sync and billing are done; we build only the CRM side.

Monthly, self-serve

Kommo — the market leader — only sells 6–24-month upfront contracts. We sell $19/month, cancel anytime, no sales call.

pilot on our own base, then a mechanical scale-or-park decision. Criteria agreed before launch.

Scale if (90 days)

  • ≥100 activated workspaces
  • retention lift in the activated no-CRM cohort
  • ≥20 paying / ≥$500 MRR on the charged cohort

Park if

  • <30 activated workspaces, or
  • <8 paying with a flat trajectory
  • → stop, write down learnings, move on
