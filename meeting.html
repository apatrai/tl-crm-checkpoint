Context: the 20.07 meeting

The Timelines CRM idea was born at the growth weekly on 2026-07-20 (Anton + Ishay, ~1h53m). Quotes below are translated from Russian; full transcript: team-ops/meetings/2026-07-20-growth-weekly-ishay-transcript.md.

How the idea came up

The discussion started from the top-of-funnel decline outside marketplaces. Anton: "there's obviously a big drop in non-marketplace, non-CRM stories." From there: "for most of these small companies, some contacts inside would be enough for them. LATAM is full of them… why don't we vibe-code our own? …spec a WhatsApp-first CRM… Then we could layer AI on top. That's what everyone wants."

Positions

Anton: a standalone app under the TimelinesAI brand, not inside the core product — "I'd call it Timelines CRM and sell it as a TimelinesAI addon… put it on the crm.timelines.ai domain… build it on our own SDK." Inside the core is a non-starter: "as soon as we do anything inside — that's a year, that's our UI, all those complications."

Ishay: backed the idea; his main objection was not the idea but the owner: "the idea is good — even if it fails, it's worth trying… My main problem here is who will own it. I keep coming back to MessageHub — nobody looks after MessageHub."

Why this is retention: Anton: "our bucket is leaking… and the leakiest segment is the people who have no CRM at all." That segment is the biggest one, and WhatsApp-first is a growing market.

What the meeting decided

  • The gate: "on Timelines CRM — let's write the spec and look at it… we'll see the scope." Scope is decided only after the spec.
  • Roles (proposed, not ratified): Anton + Cyril — business requirements via a recorded working call; Alexandra — analytics groundwork; final spec — Anton; development — Orkhan; sales — Cyril.
  • Process: not through the regular product meeting — a separate meeting: Ishay + Anton + Igor + Cyril, with Igor given the materials and the right framing in advance.
  • Benchmark: Kommo (already overgrown), plus research into acquire.com and small "WhatsApp CRM" products.
  • Technical: crm.timelines.ai domain, built on the TimelinesAI SDK/API; the WABA (official WhatsApp Business API) — per Ishay, ready "within a few weeks."

Still open after the meeting

  1. The final owner (Ishay's MessageHub concern is not resolved).
  2. Whether Igor accepts it into the R&D orbit (the "not in this quarter's roadmap" reflex is the risk).
  3. A committed WABA API date — a verbal "few weeks" is not a date.
